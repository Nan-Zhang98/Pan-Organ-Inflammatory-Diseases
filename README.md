# Pan-Organ Inflammatory Diseases

## Overview

Current research on inflammatory diseases largely remains confined to single organs, which limits our ability to elucidate the shared immunological principles underlying cross-organ inflammatory disorders. In this project, we constructed a comprehensive pan-organ single-cell transcriptomic atlas comprising over **3.87 million cells** across **8 human organs** and **29 inflammatory diseases**.

Through systematic single-cell integration, macrophage subclustering, trajectory inference, regulatory network analysis, in vivo validation, and spatial transcriptomic analysis, we identified a conserved macrophage pathogenic trajectory that links innate immune activation with adaptive immune recruitment across human inflammatory diseases.
